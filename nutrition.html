5 Days Nutritions Intake

Day 01

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with a scoop of protein powder, 1 tablespoon of almond butter, and mixed berries.
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with honey and a handful of walnuts.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken breast, quinoa, steamed broccoli, and mixed greens with olive oil.
  • Dinner: Salmon with sweet potato, sautéed asparagus, and a side of brown rice.

Day 02

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs (4 eggs) with spinach, bell peppers, and whole-grain toast.
  • Snack: Apple with 2 tablespoons of peanut butter.
  • Lunch: Turkey burger (lean ground turkey) on a whole-grain bun with avocado and a side salad.
  • Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with mixed vegetables (bell peppers, snap peas, carrots) and a side of brown rice.

Day 03

  • Breakfast: Smoothie with protein powder, spinach, banana, chia seeds, and almond milk.
  • Snack: Cottage cheese with pineapple slices.
  • Lunch: Grilled steak with mashed potatoes, green beans, and a side salad.
  • Dinner: Baked cod with couscous, roasted Brussels sprouts, and a mixed greens salad.

Day 04

  • Breakfast: Whole-grain pancakes with a side of scrambled eggs and berries.
  • Snack: Protein shake with almond milk and a small handful of mixed nuts.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken Caesar salad with a whole-grain roll.
  • Dinner: Lean pork chop with roasted sweet potatoes, sautéed spinach, and brown rice.

Day 05

  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt parfait with granola, chia seeds, and fresh fruit
  • Snack: Boiled eggs (2) and a few whole-grain crackers.
  • Lunch: Grilled shrimp with whole-grain pasta, cherry tomatoes, basil, and a light olive oil drizzle.
  • Dinner: Chicken stir-fry with mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, carrots) over jasmine rice.
